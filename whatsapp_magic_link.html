How It Works

Simple as Sending a Message

The entire login experience lives inside WhatsApp — no app switch, no password, no friction.

📱

Open WhatsApp
User searches for the Clix Bot number
💬
2
Send a Message
Say "Hi" — anything works to trigger the bot
🤖
3
Main Menu
Clix Bot shows Login & Link Mobile options
👆
4
User Chooses
Login (existing) or Link Mobile (first time)
🔗
5
Magic Link / OTP
Bot sends a secure link or email OTP to verify
✅
6
In the App!
Clix opens and the user is logged in — done
🔐
Already a Clix user?
If your WhatsApp number is already linked to your Clix account, just tap Login — you'll get a magic link and be in the app within seconds.
🔗
First time on WhatsApp?
Tap Link Mobile with Email, enter your Clix email, verify with OTP — your number is linked and future logins take one tap.
